DSR — Our Proposed Score

A distribution-aware fidelity score for validating quantum execution outputs against a known ideal.

Differential Success Rate (DSR)
$DSR = \text{clip}\!\left( \dfrac{\bar{p}_{exp} - p_{comp}}{\bar{p}_{exp} + p_{comp}},\ 0,\ 1 \right)$
Terms

$\bar{p}_{exp}$ — average probability mass across expected outcomes.

$p_{comp}$ — largest probability among non-expected (competing) outcomes.

Properties
  • Michelson contrast form — signal over noise
  • Bounded in $[0, 1]$ — higher is better
  • Distribution-aware — captures separation, not just mode hit
