qiskit_qward.validators.TeleportationValidator¶

class TeleportationValidator(payload_size=3, gates=None, use_barriers=True, save_statevector=False)[source]¶

Validator for quantum teleportation protocol. Implements the standard teleportation protocol with optional gate operations.

Initialize the teleportation validator.

Parameters:

  • payload_size (int) – Size of the payload qubit

  • gates (Union[List, int]) – List of gates to apply to the payload qubit

  • use_barriers (bool) – Whether to include barriers in the circuit

  • save_statevector (bool) – Whether to save the statevector
