Project 3 Part 2: Written Methods Section (10%) 

Student Learning Outcomes: 
•	Write an original or paraphrased methods section that includes your field’s genre characteristics 

Overview: For this project, you will use what you have learned in your genre analysis to either: 1) write an original methods section or 2) paraphrase an existing methods section

Instructions Option 1 – Original Methods Section:
1.	Write an original methods section according to the norms in your field. Minimum 500 words.
2.	Identify the appropriate moves from Project 3 Part 1 in your own text using comment boxes. 
3.	Add elements of cohesion (if and when appropriate). These can be elements of cohesion that we learned about in Module 1 and/or Module 2. Highlight them.
Instructions Option 2 – Paraphrased Methods Section: 
1.	Choose a pre-existing methods section, or part of one. This could be a methods section that you have already published, or it can be taken from another article written by someone else. Minimum 500 words.
2.	Paraphrase the methods section using a variety of techniques that were discussed in class. Your paraphrase should have the same meaning as the original but be written in a completely different way.
3.	Identify the appropriate moves from Project 3 Part 1 in your own text using comment boxes and add elements of cohesion (if and when appropriate).  Highlight them.
4.	Submit a copy of the original methodology along with your paraphrased version. I recommend using a table with two columns with one side as the original and the other side as the paraphrase.
Due Monday, Week 10
