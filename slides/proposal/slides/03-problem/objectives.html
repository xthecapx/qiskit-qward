Objectives

O1 — Circuit Footprint Definition

Identify and select the most relevant set of pre-runtime metrics that collectively define a "circuit footprint" capable of discriminating between different algorithmic paradigms.

O2 — Predictive Modeling

Develop and train a diverse set of machine learning models to map the circuit footprint to key post-runtime quality attributes, specifically execution quality (DSR, HF, TVD), across different quantum providers.

O3 — Validation

Evaluate the generalizability and soundness of the models by comparing their predicted quality attributes against ground-truth data obtained from real execution on quantum hardware, using unobserved circuit configurations that preserve the structural characteristics of the selected algorithmic primitives.
